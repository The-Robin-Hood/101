003.3n+1

  • The Collatz Conjecture, also known as the 3n+1 problem or the hailstone sequence.
  • It is a mathematical conjecture that concerns a sequence of numbers.
  • The conjecture states that if you take any positive integer, and divide it by 2 if it is even.
  • Multiply it by 3 and add 1 if it is odd.
  • Repeat this process with the resulting number, and eventually you reach the number 1.
